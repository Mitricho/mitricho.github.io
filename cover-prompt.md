Вот несколько вариантов промптов для генерации обложки. Они составлены так, чтобы изображение получилось технологичным, соответствовало названию и содержало ключевые элементы AR (очки, смартфоны, планшеты, голограммы).

Выберите тот, который лучше всего подходит под ваш визуальный стиль (реализм, минимализм, футуризм).

### Вариант 1 (Современный и реалистичный)
**Акцент на сочетании реального мира и высоких технологий.**

> **Prompt:** Photorealistic book cover design, high detail. A pair of sleek, modern Augmented Reality smart glasses rests on a wooden table in a cozy, sunlit library. Through the lenses of the glasses, a vibrant, holographic fantasy game scene projects onto the table surface: a detailed miniature dragon, floating interface elements, stats, and glowing runes. In the background, slightly blurred, a smartphone or tablet lies on the table displaying a similar AR tracking screen. The atmosphere blends the real world with the digital. Style: High-tech, clean, inspirational. No text.

### Вариант 2 (Динамичный и футуристический)
**Акцент на действии и игровом процессе.**

> **Prompt:** Epic cinematic scene for a book cover. A young game developer's hands are held out, interacting with a massive, translucent AR hologram floating in a modern office space. The hologram shows a complex 3D level design of a fantasy game, complete with characters, trees, and enemy spawn points. The person is wearing AR glasses, and reflections of code and UI elements are visible on the lenses. In the environment, multiple devices like a smartphone and a tablet lie nearby, also displaying the same AR scene from different angles. Lighting is dramatic, with cool blue and cyan glows from the hologram. Photorealistic, ultra-detailed. No text.

### Вариант 3 (Минималистичный и концептуальный)
**Акцент на пересечении реальности и виртуальности, хорошо смотрится как абстрактная обложка.**

> **Prompt:** Minimalist 3D render for a book cover. A simple, real-world smartphone floats in the center of an empty, dark grey room. The smartphone's screen is on, displaying a game character. A beam of light connects the phone to a realistic, life-sized holographic warrior standing in the middle of the room, holding a sword. The warrior looks both digital and solid. The composition is clean, with strong contrast. Style: Isometric view, smooth textures, ambient occlusion. No text.

### Вариант 4 (Крупный план и детали)
**Акцент на устройствах и интерфейсе.** 

> **Prompt:** Close-up shot for a tech book cover. A person's hand holding the latest smartphone, pointing it at a messy desk. On the smartphone screen, we see the desk transformed into a sci-fi battlefield with tiny tanks and soldiers. In the background, a pair of high-tech AR glasses is also visible, showing a different part of the same battlefield. The image is split between the blurry real world and the sharp, colorful AR overlay on the screen. Depth of field effect. Style: High contrast, vibrant colors, technological. No text.

### Вариант 5 (Абстрактный/Глитч)
**Акцент на коде и технологиях.**

> **Prompt:** Abstract book cover design representing Augmented Reality game development. In the center, a glowing 3D polygonal game character is dissolving into particles. The particles are flowing into the camera lens of a smartphone and the sensors of AR glasses placed at the bottom corners of the image. Background consists of digital rain, flowing lines of code (matrix style), and circuit board patterns. The style is a mix of glitch art and cyberpunk. No text.

**Совет:** Для более точного результата к любому из этих промптов можно добавить уточнение **"8k resolution, unreal engine 5 render, octane render"**
